Square Enix Final Fantasy VII

Final Fantasy VII is the seventh main installment in the Final Fantasy series released in 1997 and is one of the most iconic titles under Square Enix. The title has achieved critical success and has even been given the title for one of the greatest video games of all time. Square Enix will be releasing a remake of this title early 2020 and will feature a completely enhanced look using full polygonal graphics and a new battle system.

The remake is something that is most pleasing for nostalgia. To those who have never played Final Fantasy VII before, this will still be an enjoyable experience for those who enjoy a good story based game, a great soundtrack, and a battle system similar to another title, Kingdom Hearts.
